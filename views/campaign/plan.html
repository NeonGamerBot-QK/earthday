Plan for earth day


Our Idea

Our ideas are to make birdhouses out of Recycled plastic, Cardboard, Leaves + twigs and glue.

The general idea is to make an advisory birdhouse for each advisory.

Our research will include what the best recycled materials would be to use.

The plan for our earth day activity is for each advisory makes bird houses

Our resources

The resources we will use includes: Recycled plastic. Cardboard. Leaves, twigs and glue

To buy all any of the materials it shall cost 0$

Cardboard - we can collect it over the next 2 months Recycled plastic - we can collect it Everything else - we already have it at school/we can collect it

Planning

The planning that would need to be done ahead of time would be setting of the material in adviser. The activity will take 45 mins.

This event will take 35 min building and 10 min show & see.
